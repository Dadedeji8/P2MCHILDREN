About Us

P2MChildren is a leading non-governmental organization saddled with the responsibility of Educating, Empowering and Protecting Children, especially the Girl Child and Women in Nigeria. P2MChildren is committed to realizing the formal education of Children, especially the “GIRL CHILD”, through our GIRL CHILD EDUCATION and SKILL ACQUISITION schemes in North Eastern and North Western Nigeria to curtail early marriages and reduce the burden of illiteracy amongst the female gender in these parts of the Country. We are set to ensure that, every Girl child has access to quality education as it is a fundamental human right in acquiring at least a minimum of Secondary School Education in this region where there is poor/low level of education/literacy. The skill acquistion excercises/programs is to empower girls and women to become self dependent and resourceful members of the society. The P2Mchildren SEX EDUCATION and CHILD ABUSE programs, is/are i ncooperated into the conventional School system in the South, East and Western Nigeria. This initiative will reduce; the menace of teenage/unwanted pregnancies, Maternal and Child mortality, and child abuse in these targeted places where there are high reports of abuse cases. read more

Intervention

  • Financial Support/Scholarship Schemes.

  • Communication through: advocacy/awareness creation/sensitization/social Mobilization Programs in targeted communities.

  • Skill Acquisition/Empowerment Programs for Women.

  • Incorporation of sex education into the conventional school system, in the South, East and Western states in Nigeria.

  • Available call lines and referral of cases of abuse/molestation and domestic violence in collaboration/partnership with other Organizations for the protection of the Girl-Child and Women in Nigeria.

  • Male involvement programs: to increase demand for the female-Child education and support in the North.

  • Training Teachers on the management of children with mental disorder/special needs.
