Story Five

What do you have to say about a 15 Year old teenager, a sweet girl found roaming in a village around Magare, in Kebbi State? She had no slippers on and was hungrily looking for Mangoes to pluck from nearby trees for the afternoon fill.

Unschooled and roaming girls like Aisha are vulnerable to early marriages, physical and sexual abuse.

Project 2 Million Children Back to School Initiative is a Non Governmental Organization ready to sponsor such girls back to School.
