ABOUT US

P2MChildren is a leading non-governmental organization saddled with the responsibility of Educating,Empowering and Protecting Children,especially the Girl Child and Women in Nigeria. P2MChildren is committed to realizing the formal education of Children, especially the “GIRL CHILD”, through our GIRL CHILD EDUCATION and SKILL ACQUISITION schemes in North Eastern and North Western Nigeria to curtail early marriages and reduce the burden of illiteracy amongst the girls and women in Nigeria, by ensuring access to quality primary and secondary school education .

Our Mission

To provide cross cutting-edge, innovative and sustainable approaches to mitigating poor education and poverty alleviation in Nigeria through effective service delivery/scholarship schemes, capacity building, strategic information and demand creation/advocacy in partnership with other stakeholders.

Our Goal

Ensuring that every female in Nigeria, especially the “girl-child” in the far North is thriving, learning and protected! All our actions contribute to the sponsorship/scholarship and enpowerment for; >

  • Quality education and digital literacy
  • Ensuring economic and financial inclusivity
  • Covering gender gaps
  • Medical care impacting maternal and mental health of the girl child and women in Nigeria achieving the Sustainable Development Goals.

WHAT WE DO

Our story

  • Children’s education, empowerment and protection initiative, as every child as right to be protected from violence, abuse and exploitation.

  • Focusing on GIRL CHILD EDUCATION in Northern parts of Nigeria, especially the North Eastern and North Western Nigeria where the level of literacy is very poor.

  • Fear and anxiety management in Children/Mental Health Programs.

  • Fight against abuse and domestic violence in Children and Women in the country.

    • love

  • Incorporating SEX EDUCATION and CHILD ABUSE programs in Schools (Western, Eastern and Southern Parts of Nigeria).

  • Providing guidance and counseling on career choice, menstrual hygiene and cycle.

  • Tracking and Follow-up on students who dropped out of school and IDPs for a return to school.

  • Training teachers on managing students especially those with special needs like dyslexia.

    • Charity

  • Empowerment through skill acquisition activities in Schools, Villages and in strategic places where services are needed, especially for non working class women in all states across the federation; Fashion designing, hair making, decorations, catering services, soap making, insecticides etc

  • Help women acquire more knowledge on FGM/STDs, natural birth spacing methods through menstrual cycle reading and importance of breast feeding children.
